Our Story

Born from a Love for Real Desi Food

Dhaba Square started with one simple belief — that the best food doesn't need a five-star setting. It needs the right masalas, the right fire, and the right hands.

We brought the warmth of a Punjab highway dhaba to the heart of Whitefield — khatiya seating, clay-pot curries, tandoori smoke, and all. Today, across two branches in Nallurhalli and Kundalahalli, we serve the same food your dadi would recognize.
